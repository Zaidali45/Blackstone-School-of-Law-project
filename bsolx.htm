Approved Study Center

Blackstone school of Law & Business is a recognised centre from Pearson Board UK. Blackstone has a proud history of innovation in the field of legal and business education in Lahore, Pakistan. Since its inception the college has been termed as one of the best law and business college for students who are looking for a more personalised skill based education. Our mission is to prepare 21st century lawyers and business professionals who can create a social impact in the filed of law, business and psychology. Our graduates have been able to secure positions at prestigious law firms, have became barristers, started their career in media, software houses, educational institutes, and secured admission as top UK universities for their LLM and Bar degrees.
