Programmes Offered

LLB (HONS) - UNIVERSITY OF LONDON

LLB (Hons) from University of London is a Qualifying Law Degree which means our graduates are directly eligible to undertake the vocational stage to become a Barrister with the Bar of England & Wales. After the University of London LLB (Hons), our students complete a 9 month Bar Professional Training Course (BPTC) at one of the top ranking universities in the UK or take 9 month Bar Transfer Course at Blackstone School of Law (Lahore campus or online). After successful completion of their exams, they become entitled to the title of “Barrister”. Please note: The local Pakistani 5-year LLB is NOT a Qualifying Law Degree. The graduates of local Pakistani LLB are not eligible to undertake Bar Professional Training Course (BPTC) in the UK to become a Barrister. Whereas, the University of London LLB (Hons) makes the candidates directly eligible to undertake BPTC to become a Barrister.


BAR TRANSFER COURSE (BTC) IN PAKISTAN

Blackstone School of Law & Business takes pride in successfully preparing Barristers in Pakistan. Our Bar Transfer Course is a 9 month programme where lawyers and law graduates undertake preparatory classes at Blackstone and appear for their exams in the UK to become Barristers. One of the hallmarks of our Bar Transfer Course is our outstanding faculty who are all Barristers themselves.


Stages of Bar Transfer Course include: Classes for all four subjects at Blackstone School of Law, application for exemptions from Bar Standard Board (UK), Membership with the Honourable Society of Lincoln’s Inn, undertaking exams in the UK, and finally, Call to the Bar. All external and local law lawyers are eligible for this course.
